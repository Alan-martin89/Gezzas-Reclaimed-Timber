Welcome to Gezza's Reclaimed Timber! Here I strive to make the most from all timbers! This small business dedicates itself to making items from Reclaimed timber. My name is Gerald and I founded this company in May of 2009. I have hopes of making anything I can from good reclaimed wood. Based in a small town called Totnes within the South of Devon I have made everything from a thunderbox (garden eco-toilet) to birdtables and garden sheds to garage doors. In some circumstances where wood must be purchased for the job, this is fine by me but that doesn't mean we can't do our bit by grabbing some leftovers from that D.I.Y job last summer! Please feel free to browse the gallery to see my previous work that has been carried out.
