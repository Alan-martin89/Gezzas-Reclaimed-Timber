About us

Save the world!
Our main mission is to build, Build a new world out of the old. We strive to give life to something which everyone else has given up on and that is wood itself! With the world in economic crisis our mission is to do our bit to help where we can.

Built to order
Every item at Gezza's is handmade by Gezza himself, with love and care to make every customer happy with their bespoke order. With over 30 years of Carpentry experience, nearly 10 years of being self employed and all the tools needed.

Security
As above, we stated that we use reclaimed timber. But sometimes due to safety and security, this is just not possible. In this situation we do order suitable products to finish your job with customer communication a key factor 100% of the time
